About me

About

A neuroscientist and researcher by training, I'm passionate about working at the nexus of Data Science and Neural Systems. Currently, I'm working on continuing the research from my MSc with the Institute of Neuroinformatics. For a short summary of my academic work, check out my Bachelor's thesis proposal from New York University and the report we published in Nature Neuroscience for that project, and an abstract of my Master's thesis in ETH Zürich and University of Zürich for the Swiss Society of Neuroscience 2019 Annual Meeting.

Simulation of Cortical Neurons

MSc Thesis with ETH Zürich and University of Zürich: Synaptic background activity and short-term plasticity optimize synaptic information transfer between Layer 2/3 cortical neurons.

Recommender System and Sentiment Analysis for Aibrnb

Built a system than generates personalised recommendations for Airbnb users based on sentiment polarity of their and other users' comments. For more details, check out my Github repo, my article on Medium, or this dashboard I created on estimated sentiment polarity data.

Anomaly Detection of Lorenz Attractor model data

Trained a feed forward neural net (using Tensorflow) on extracted frequency spectrum to detect anomalous data (see Github repo). Will be extended in future to work for unsupervised anomaly detection problems using an autoencoder trained with an LSTM.

Simulations of Auditory, Visual, and Vestibular Sensory Systems

Computer simulations of a cochlear implant, a retinal implant, and a vestibular implant (see Github repo). Completed in collaboration with colleagues from ETH Zürich.
